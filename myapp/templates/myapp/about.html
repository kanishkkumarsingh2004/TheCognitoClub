About The Cognito Club

Pioneering AI and ML Innovation at Jain (Deemed-to-be University) FET

At The Cognito Club, we're more than just a student group—we're a movement. Based at JAIN (Deemed-to-be-University), we're a thriving community where creativity catches fire, ideas turn into action, and students from every corner of the university come together to shape the future. No matter if you're a fresher finding your footing, a final-year trailblazer, or anywhere in between—tech geek or creative soul—our doors are flung wide open for you.

We're here to ignite your spark through immersive workshops, bold projects, mind-bending challenges like hackathons and innovation showdowns, and electrifying networking opportunities. Passionate about solving puzzles? Obsessed with the next big tech breakthrough? Or maybe you just love connecting with people who think big? This is your space to experiment, collaborate, and shine alongside a crew of like-minded visionaries.

Who We Are

The Cognito Club at JAIN (Deemed-to-be-University), Bangalore, is a powerhouse of innovation, creativity, and technology. We are a dynamic student-led community passionate about AI, ML, and emerging technologies. Through cutting-edge events, hands-on workshops, and groundbreaking projects, we empower students to push boundaries and shape the future of technology. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academia and industry, providing students with the tools, knowledge, and exposure needed to excel in an ever-evolving tech landscape. With a reach of 2,500–3,000 JAIN University students and 4,000–5,500 across Bangalore universities, we’re revolutionizing the future of tech through collaboration, learning, and innovation!
